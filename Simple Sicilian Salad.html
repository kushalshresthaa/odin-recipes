Simple Sicilian Salad Dressing

home

Simple Sicilian salad dressing is a bright, zesty blend that captures the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean. It's typically made with high-quality extra virgin olive oil as the base, offering a smooth, fruity richness. Red wine vinegar or lemon juice adds a tangy kick, while a touch of garlic and a sprinkle of dried oregano or basil brings depth and herbal notes.

A pinch of salt and freshly cracked black pepper round it all off, balancing the acidity with savory warmth. This dressing is light yet flavorful, perfect for drizzling over crisp greens, tomatoes, and olives, embodying the simple yet bold spirit of Sicilian cuisine.
