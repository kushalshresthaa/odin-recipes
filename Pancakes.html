Easy Pancakes

home

Description

Pancakes are fluffy, golden-brown discs of comfort food, often enjoyed as a breakfast treat but perfect for any time of day. They have a soft, airy texture on the inside, with a slightly crisp exterior when cooked just right.

The flavor is subtly sweet, but it's the toppings that take them to the next level—whether it's a drizzle of maple syrup, a dollop of whipped cream, fresh berries, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. Each bite is warm, light, and satisfying, making pancakes the perfect canvas for creativity in the kitchen, with endless variations like chocolate chips, banana slices, or even savory options.

    Ingredients
  • Flour: This quick pancake recipe starts with a cup of all-purpose flour.
  • Sugar: You’ll need two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Baking powder: Baking powder acts as a leavening agent, ensuring perfectly fluffy pancakes.
  • Salt: Salt enhances the overall flavor of the pancakes, but it won’t make the pancakes taste salty.
  • Milk & Oil: Milk and oil add moisture and flavor to the pancake batter.
  • Egg: A beaten egg lends more moisture and helps bind the batter together.
    Steps
  1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook on the other side.
